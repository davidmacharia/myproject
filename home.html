Ardhi House in Nyandarua County, located in Ol Kalou town, was established to bring land registry services closer to the residents of Nyandarua. Previously, residents had to travel to Nyahururu in Laikipia County for services.

Funded by the national government at a cost of KSh 17 million, Ardhi House was completed in mid-2019. It addresses inefficiencies experienced at the Nyahururu registry, serving Nyandarua and Samburu Counties.

The fully automated facility allows for online searches, improving service delivery and significantly reducing the time required for land-related services, bringing relief to the community.

At Ardhi House, we provide quality services to satisfy your needs. Visit us at Ol Kalou near Hunduma Center or check our official website:
